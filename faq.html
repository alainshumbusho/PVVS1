Can I resend voter notifications to voters?

Yes, but only to individual voters. It is not possible to resend all e-mails at the same time.

?

Can I send voter invitations manually by e-mail?

Yes you can. First you have to upload your electoral roll or the number of required access accounts to the Online Voting Manager. You can then export the login data generated by the system and manually send it to each member by e-mail. Each member should receive a voter ID, a password, and an election URL. Please note that for data protection reasons we first have to activate the option to download the login data.

?

Can the e-mail address of an eligible voter be changed during the election?

If an e-mail address has a mistake in it, you have the option to send the voter credentials to each eligible voter again: the e-mail address can be the same, corrected, or even completely different.
This feature is only available during the voting period on the “Eligible voters” tab.

?

Can voters be reached by phone if they don't have an e-mail address?

You as an election organizer are welcome to organize this: if you do not have an e-mail address, you can reach each member by phone and pass on their login data in that way. You can manage and export the login data in the Online Voting Manager. Please note that for data protection reasons we first have to activate the option to download the login data.

?

Do upper and lower case letters matter in the e-mail address?

Yes, the VVS Online Voting System always works case-sensitive. The applicable information is that which you entered into your electoral roll. If this contains F.Surname@mailprovider.com, it would have to be entered in the same way when logging in—assuming that the e-mail address has been set as a voter ID.
